Chickpea Salad

Chickpea Salad is a nutritious and refreshing dish made with chickpeas, fresh vegetables, and a tangy lemon dressing. It's perfect as a light lunch or a side dish and packed with protein and fiber.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the Vegetables:
    • Dice the cucumber, bell pepper, and onion. Halve the cherry tomatoes.
    • Chop the parsley and cilantro.
  2. Combine Ingredients:
    • In a large bowl, combine the chickpeas, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, cherry tomatoes, parsley, and cilantro.
  3. Prepare the Dressing:
    • In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, cumin powder, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Mix and Serve:
    • Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
    • Adjust seasoning if needed.
    • Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator before serving.
