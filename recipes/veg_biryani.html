Vegetable Biryani

Vegetable Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish cooked with mixed vegetables, spices, and herbs. It's a one-pot meal that's both satisfying and nutritious, perfect for special occasions or everyday dining.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, minced
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit or chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, potatoes), chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fried onions, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Rice:
    • Rinse 1 cup of basmati rice until the water runs clear.
    • Soak rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Cook the Vegetables:
    • Heat oil or ghee in a large pot over medium heat.
    • Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
    • Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
    • Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© until aromatic.
    • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
    • Add mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
    • Stir in turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
    • Add yogurt and cook for a few more minutes.
  3. Combine and Cook:
    • Add the drained rice to the pot with the vegetables.
    • Pour in 2 cups of water and bring to a boil.
    • Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
    • Stir in chopped cilantro and mint leaves.
  4. Serve:
    • Garnish with fried onions if desired.
    • Serve hot with raita or yogurt on the side.
